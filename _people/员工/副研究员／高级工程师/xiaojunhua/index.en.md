## Biography

* Research interests: computer architecture, high-performance processor design, and performance analysis and evaluation
* Department: Microprocessor Technology Research Center, State Key Laboratory of Processor Chips
* Advisor category: Master's supervisor in Computer System Architecture
* Contact: xiaojunhua@ict.ac.cn

## Education and Experience

1. Received a Ph.D. degree in engineering from the Institute of Computing Technology in 2008.
2. Currently serves as a senior engineer at the Institute of Computing Technology.
3. His work focuses on computer architecture and microprocessor design, including processor architecture, performance analysis, logic design, and forward-looking processor technologies for the Loongson series.
